Our Story

Waves Women's Salon

Waves Women's Salon is Thiruporur's premier destination for women's beauty, hair care, and fashion. Nestled conveniently on the Chengalpattu–Thiruporur Road, our salon offers a welcoming and luxurious environment where every woman is treated like royalty. With a perfect 5-star rating from our valued clients, we take immense pride in delivering exceptional quality in every service we provide.

From precision haircuts and vibrant coloring to skincare treatments and the latest fashion wear, Waves Women's Salon is your one-stop beauty destination. Our skilled team of beauty professionals stays up to date with the latest trends and techniques to ensure you always walk out feeling confident and refreshed. Open every day of the week from 10 AM to 9 PM, we are always here to serve you at your convenience.
